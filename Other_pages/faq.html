Frequently Asked Questions

What is BriefIT?

BriefIT is a tool that allows you to summarize any text with just a click.

How does it work?

You can paste the text you want to summarize, and BriefIT will generate a summary for you.

Is it free to use?

Yes, BriefIT offers free access to its basic features.

Can I customize the summary length?

Yes, you can adjust the summary length using the provided slider.
