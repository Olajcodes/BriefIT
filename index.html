About Us

BriefIT is an AI Driven Article/News summarization platform that condenses extensive news content into easily absorbable briefs, our mission is to deliver essential information while optimizing user time and understanding.

BriefIT came into existence when a body of ten individuals, united by their passion for innovation and simplification,came together to harness the power of AI and revolutionize how people consume news, bridging the gap between information overload and timely understanding

These ten individuals, with diverse expertise and a shared vision, formed a cohesive team that;

  • Worked tirelessly to bring BriefIT to life
  • Dreamed of making news consumption more efficient
  • Believed in the potential of AI to simplify complex information
  • Committed to continuously improving and refining the technology
  • Shared a common goal of empowering users with concise, accurately, and timely briefs.

Our collaboration and dedication gave birth to BriefIT, a pioneering news summarizing platform that transform the way we interact with news.

Our Mission

BriefIT is Committed to harnessing the power of artificial intelligence to deliver succinct, trustworthy and timely news summaries, streamlining the information gathering process and fostering a more informed and engaged community.

Our Vision

To revolutionize the way people interact with news, creating a world where everyone has access to timely, accurate and concise information empowering individuals to make informed decisions, think critically and shape a better future.
